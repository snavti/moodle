Teacher Manual
==============

Your Moodle administrator has installed the StudentQuiz Plugin to your school's 
Moodle Website. This guide explains how to configure a StudentQuiz activity
to be added into your course, how you can encourage and moderate your students
to contribute question to the question pool, what statistics you can draw from 
your students activties and how you can recycle the question pool of one course 
to another.
